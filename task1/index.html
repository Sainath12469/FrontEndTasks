Welcome to the CVR Student Management Portal

CVR College of Engineering has been granted autonomous status by the UGC. CVR set a record by getting this status in the shortest time (less than 12.5 years since the establishment) among 300+ colleges in the entire Telangana (Osmania University) area. CVR continues to create records UGC Autonomous The college was granted Autonomous Status by the UGC from the academic year 2014–15 to 2019-2020 for a period of six years. This was extended for a period five years from the academic year 2020-2021 to 2024-2025 and again extended to ten years from academic year 2025-26 to 2034-2035. CVR had set a record by achieving this status in the shortest time (less than 12.5 years since the establishment of the college) among 300+ colleges in the entire Telangana (Osmania University) area. Earlier in 2011-12, CVR had become the youngest (10-year-old) college to get the coveted JNTUH Autonomous status for 3 years (2011-12 to 2013-14).Our Director Dr. K. Rama Sastri and Principal Dr. A.D. Rajkumar receiving the order in 2011 from Director I/c, AAC, JNTUH Dr. T. Kishen Kumar Reddy
